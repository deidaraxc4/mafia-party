How to play Mafia help

Mafia is a game of deceit and trickery. As a town it is your job to find and kill all of the Mafia and any Neutral evil roles. As the Mafia it is your job to take control of the village by getting rid of everyone else as you choose together who to assassinate. As a Neutral role, your win condition varies, but generally you are not on any particular side, but look out for yourself.
